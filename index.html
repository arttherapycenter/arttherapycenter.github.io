Children Art Therapy Centre

At the moment, one in six children currently live in conflict areas, meaning that 357 million children have experienced or are suffering different levels of assault and danger. This hypothesis seeks to explore the impact of exposure to war-traumatic experiences on children's mental health. Their mental health is on the verge of danger, as they have experienced and seen things that no kids should have, their homes have been bombed and their loved ones killed. When an observer in a war zone, instead of being passive bystanders, we should look at opportunities to change the situation.

  • Stage 1

    "Trauma does not ordinarily get better by it self it burrows down further and further under the child's defenses and coping strategies"

  • Stage 2

    ART IS DEPPER THAN WORDS.

  • Stage 3

    Gives them a moment to themselves to process their situation and relate to eachother through their art and stories with the presence of a specialized ArtPsychotherapist.
